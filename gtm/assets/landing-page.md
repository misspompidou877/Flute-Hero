## 3. How it works — 3 steps

**Section heading:** Three steps, five minutes to the first song

1. **She blows a note.** Flute Hero listens through the device microphone — nothing is
   recorded or sent anywhere. On-screen, she sees a fingering diagram and a live tuning
   meter.
2. **Piper tells her when she's in tune.** Green glow when she's spot on, a warm nudge
   when she's close. No red, no buzzers, no failing grade.
3. **She unlocks the next song.** Each mastered note opens a new song in Songkeeper's
   Sky — the games' story world. Progress is saved on the device automatically.

**Micro-CTA under this section:** Try Level 1 free — no card, no signup

## 4. Features

Short, benefit-led, one line each. Grid or list layout.

- **Real-time pitch listening** — hears every note through the device mic and shows
  in-tune/close/off, instantly, while she plays.
- **Flute-specific fingering diagrams** — hand-drawn, not generic instrument-agnostic
  charts; built for concert flute fingerings from the first note.
- **An 8-level curriculum** — First Breath through Sky High, each level gated on
  actually playing the note correctly, not just tapping "next."
- **Real sheet music, playable songs** — proper notation, not just games, from Hot Cross
  Buns to Scarborough Fair.
- **A world she wants to come back to** — Songkeeper's Sky turns practice into finding
  lost songs, with Piper as a guide, not a scorekeeper.
- **Foundations for total beginners** — note reading, embouchure, and tonguing basics
  for kids who've never held a flute.
- **No ads, no chat, no strangers** — nothing in the app talks to your child except
  Piper.

**Section heading:** Built so nothing about your child leaves the device

**Body copy:**
> The microphone audio Flute Hero listens to is processed **on the device, in the

## 9. FAQ

**Is this a subscription?**
No. Flute Hero is a one-time purchase of $24.99. There's no recurring charge and nothing
to cancel.

**What do we get for free?**
Level 1 (First Breath) is free forever — three notes, two full songs, no account, no
time limit. Every new visitor also gets a 10-day trial with every level unlocked, so you
can see the whole app before deciding.

**Does the app record my child?**
No. The microphone is used to detect pitch in real time, on the device, and nothing is
recorded, saved, or sent anywhere. See the privacy section above for the full detail.

**Do I need to create an account?**
No. There's no login, no email required to use the app. Progress is saved in the
browser on the device you're using.

**What if we practise on more than one device (tablet and phone)?**
One purchase activates on up to 3 devices, so a family can use it on, for example, an
iPad and a parent's phone.

**Is this a replacement for a private flute teacher?**
No — it's built to sit alongside lessons, giving your child real-time feedback on the
days there isn't a teacher in the room. Many families use it exactly for the gap between
weekly lessons.
