# Flute Hero — Organic Social Kit

**Status:** scripts and concepts, ready to shoot/design once real footage and consented
families exist. Every "kid-progress" script below is a **template** for real footage —
it is not a script performed by an actor standing in for a fabricated testimonial. No
video may present a stock clip, an AI-generated child, or an unconsented recording as a
genuine Flute Hero user. Any kid appearing on camera requires `NEEDS_REAL_PERSON` +
signed release, per `gtm/GTM_PLAN.md` §3 Channel 2.

**Voice:** warm, plain, parent-facing captions even where the visual is kid-facing.
Piper mentions in scripts refer to the in-app mascot, not a live performer. No prices or
purchase language in any visual a child would see in-app; captions aimed at the parent
audience may mention the free Level 1 / one-time price.

## 10 short-form video scripts (kid-progress format, 15–30 sec)

Each follows the same honest arc: real kid, real mic, real progress — beginning
struggle/uncertainty → the app's real-time feedback → a small, real win. No scripted
dialogue is put in a child's mouth; captions carry the narrative instead.

**Script 1 — "Day 1 vs Day 5"**
- Beat 1 (0–5s): Kid picks up flute, unsure, plays a shaky note. On-screen caption: "Day 1."
- Beat 2 (5–15s): Quick cuts of practice — tuning meter reacting, Piper's `listening` state.
- Beat 3 (15–25s): Kid plays a clean phrase of a real song. Caption: "Day 5. Same kid."
- End card: "Flute Hero. Level 1 is free." Logo + CTA.

**Script 2 — "The first time she nailed a note"**
- Beat 1: Close-up on tuning meter, needle drifting, warm-yellow "close" state.
- Beat 2: Kid adjusts, meter shifts to green "in tune," Piper `celebrate` bounce/confetti.
- Beat 3: Quick reaction shot (kid's genuine smile — only if consented and natural,
  never staged/coached to perform surprise).
- End card: "It hears every note. Try Level 1 free."

**Script 3 — "Mic myth-buster" (screen recording, no kid needed)**
- Beat 1: Screen recording of the app listening to a played note, meter responding live.
- Caption overlay: "This is happening on the device. Nothing is recorded. Nothing is
  uploaded."
- End card: "Private by design. Try Level 1 free."

**Script 4 — "What band kids actually need"**
- Beat 1: Kid holding a school band folder/fingering chart, looking a little lost.
- Beat 2: Same kid using Flute Hero, tuning meter guiding them.
- Caption: "The lesson is 30 minutes. The other 6 days are up to her."
- End card: "Free classroom access for teachers — link in bio."

**Script 5 — "Play it for a grown-up"**
- Beat 1: Kid finishes a song in-app, badge/gem animation plays.
- Beat 2: Kid plays the song live for a parent (off-app), parent reacting genuinely.
- Caption: "The moment every parent is waiting for."
- End card: "Start free. Level 1, no card, no account."

**Script 6 — "Foundations for total beginners"**
- Beat 1: Kid who has never held a flute, on the Foundations track (embouchure practice).
- Beat 2: Quick progression through note-reading / tonguing basics.
- Caption: "Never held a flute before? This starts there."
- End card: "Free to start."

**Script 7 — "Songkeeper's Sky" world tour (screen recording)**
- Beat 1: Screen recording panning across the sky-map level landmarks.
- Caption: "8 levels. Real songs. One story world."
- End card: "First Breath → Sky High. Try it free."

**Script 8 — "No red X" (concept/animation, no kid needed)**
- Beat 1: Split screen — a generic app showing a harsh red error state (illustrative,
  not naming a competitor) vs. Flute Hero's gentle amber/encourage state.
- Caption: "Kids don't need to be told they're wrong. They need to be shown what's next."
- End card: "Gentle by design. Try Level 1 free."

**Script 9 — "One payment, no subscription" (parent-facing, animation/screen recording)**
- Beat 1: Screen recording of the pricing card in the parent zone.
- Caption: "$24.99. Once. Not $9.99 a month forever."
- End card: "See pricing."

**Script 10 — "A week of progress" (montage, needs consented family across several days)**
- Beat 1–4: Short clips from real practice sessions across a week, dated captions
  ("Monday," "Wednesday," "Friday").
- Beat 5: Final clip — noticeably more confident playing.
- Caption: "One week. Five minutes a day."
- End card: "Try Level 1 free."
