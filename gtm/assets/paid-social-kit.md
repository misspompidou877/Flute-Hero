# Flute Hero — Paid Social Kit (Meta)

**Status:** copy-ready ad concepts for Channel 2 in `gtm/GTM_PLAN.md` (parent-focused
paid social with kid-progress video creative). No spend without real creative assets and
signed consent for any kid footage (see note at the end).

**Consistency check:** price = $24.99 one-time, trial = 10 days full access, Level 1
free forever. Every variant below matches those numbers. Teacher-voice, not hype-voice —
no fake urgency, no countdown clocks, no invented reviews.

5 concepts × 3 copy variants each = 15 total. Each variant: **Hook / Body / CTA.**

---

## Concept 1 — "First song in 5 minutes"

**Why it works:** speaks to the parent's real fear — that this will be another app that
sits unopened. Leads with speed and ease, not features.

### Variant 1A
- **Hook:** She can play a real song in about 5 minutes. No flute experience needed — hers or yours.
- **Body:** Flute Hero listens while she plays and shows her, note by note, when she's in tune. Level 1 is free — no card, no account, no download of anything sketchy. Just open it and blow.
- **CTA:** Try Level 1 free

### Variant 1B
- **Hook:** From "I've never touched a flute" to a finished song — in one sitting.
- **Body:** Piper hears every note through the device mic and guides her to the next one. It's built for total beginners, with a Foundations track for kids who've never held the instrument. Free to start.
- **CTA:** Start free — no signup

### Variant 1C
- **Hook:** Most kids give up on flute practice in the first week. Flute Hero is built to get her a win in the first five minutes.
- **Body:** Real-time pitch feedback means she knows she's doing it right, immediately — not three weeks and one frustrated lesson later.
- **CTA:** See how it works

## Production notes

- All kid footage used in ads requires a real, consented family and a signed release —
  `NEEDS_REAL_PERSON` per `gtm/GTM_PLAN.md` §3 Channel 2. No stock footage presented as
  a real user, no AI-generated "kid" shown as a testimonial.
- No star ratings, review counts, or "as seen in" logos may be added to any creative
  until they are real and current.
- No countdown timers, "X spots left," or artificial urgency — consistent with Gate 3
  (parent-trust) and the brand's "trust is the strategy" stance.
