## One-page overview (PDF-ready content)

Formatted for a single-page handout/PDF a teacher can forward to their department or
print for a parent night. Structure below; design/layout to follow `STYLE_GUIDE.md` teal
palette when produced as an actual PDF.

**Header:** Flute Hero — for your students, between lessons

**Sub-line:** A free classroom tool that listens while students practise flute, and
tells them when they're in tune.

**What it is (3 short bullets):**
- A browser-based app (works on any tablet or phone with a microphone) that gives
  real-time pitch feedback for beginner flute.
- An 8-level curriculum — First Breath, Finding My Voice, Middle Path, Reaching Higher,
  Taking Flight, Sharps & Flats, Filling the Gaps, Sky High — with real fingering
  diagrams and real songs.
- Built for beginners aged roughly 7–12, including a Foundations track (note reading,
  embouchure, tonguing) for students who've never held a flute.

**Why teachers use it (3 short bullets):**
- Gives students something better than silence or guesswork on the days between
  lessons or band rehearsals.
- No account required for students — nothing for you to administer per-student.
- Private by design: microphone audio is processed on the device only and never leaves
  it — nothing to explain to a cautious parent.

**Pricing, plainly stated:**
- Families: 10-day free trial with everything unlocked, then Level 1 stays free
  forever; the rest is a one-time $24.99 — never a subscription.
- Classrooms: free access for your students while the program is in beta (see terms
  below).

**Call to action:** `NEEDS_REAL_DATA — real signup link / contact` to get classroom
access set up.

**Footer line:** No invented reviews or claims appear on this handout — everything
above is a description of what the product actually does.
