## Full description

> **Flute Hero is the flute tutor that listens.**
>
> Most beginner flute apps show a fingering chart and hope for the best. Flute Hero
> listens to your child play — through the device microphone, in real time — and shows
> them, note by note, whether they're in tune. No teacher in the room required.
>
> **Built for beginners aged 7–12, starting school band or their first lessons.**
>
> — **Piper hears every note.** Real-time pitch detection shows in-tune, close, or off —
> gently, never with a red X or a buzzer.
> — **Flute-specific from note one.** Hand-drawn fingering diagrams built for concert
> flute, not a generic instrument-agnostic chart.
> — **An 8-level curriculum.** First Breath → Finding My Voice → Middle Path → Reaching
> Higher → Taking Flight → Sharps & Flats → Filling the Gaps → Sky High. Each note is
> gated on actually playing it correctly, not just tapping "next."
> — **Real songs, real sheet music.** From Hot Cross Buns to Scarborough Fair, with
> proper notation, not just a game.
> — **A world worth coming back to.** Songkeeper's Sky turns daily practice into finding
> lost songs, with Piper as a warm, encouraging guide.
> — **Built for total beginners too.** A Foundations track covers note reading,
> embouchure, and tonguing for kids who've never held a flute.
>
> **Private by design.** The microphone is processed on the device only — audio is never
> recorded, uploaded, or stored. There's no account, no login, no chat, no ads on the
> parts your child uses.
>
> **One payment. No subscription.** Level 1 (First Breath) is free forever — three
> notes, two songs, no card, no account. Every new user also gets a 10-day trial with the
> full curriculum unlocked. After that, one $24.99 payment unlocks everything, forever.
> No recurring charge, no auto-renew, nothing to remember to cancel.
>
> Flute Hero is built to sit alongside private lessons and school band, giving your
> child real, structured feedback on the days there isn't a teacher in the room.
