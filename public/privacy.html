Privacy

Last updated [DATE]. Plain-English summary — this is how Flute Hero actually works.

Flute Hero is built to be private by design. Your child doesn't need an account, and their playing never leaves the device.

The microphone is used only to detect pitch, live, on the device. Audio is never recorded, saved, or uploaded — to us or to anyone.

No account, no sign-up

There is no login, no email required to play, and no user profile. You can open Flute Hero and start straight away.

What stays on the device

Your child's progress — levels, mastered notes, streaks and badges — is stored locally in the browser (localStorage) on your own device. It isn't sent to a server, and it isn't visible to us.

Microphone

When your child plays, the browser analyses the sound in real time to work out which note it is and whether it's in tune. That analysis happens entirely on the device and is discarded moment to moment. Nothing is stored or transmitted.

No ads, no third-party trackers

Flute Hero does not show ads and does not embed third-party advertising or tracking. [If you later add privacy-friendly, anonymised analytics, describe it here — otherwise state "we use no analytics."]

Payments

If you choose to unlock the full app, payment is handled by Lemon Squeezy as the merchant of record. They process the transaction under their own privacy policy. Unlocking works via a license key; the licence check is stateless and stores nothing about your child.

Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)
